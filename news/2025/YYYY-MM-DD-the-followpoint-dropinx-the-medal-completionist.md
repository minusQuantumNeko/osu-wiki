Before we get to Dropinx's insane achievement we believe it's important to talk about just what are *medals* and tell you a little about their history.
First 4 medals (originally "achievements") released on August 1st, 2008 and were 500, 750, 1000 and 2000 combo medals. But today [Medals](/wiki/Medals) are achievements which are given for accomplishing different gameplay goals, not just combo.
As of 16 October 2025 there are 339 medals, spread across 4 game modes and 8 categories: Beatmap Packs, Beatmap Challenge Packs, Skill, Dedication, Mod Introduction, Hush-Hush (and its Expert subcategory), Beatmap Spotlights and Seasonal Spotlights.
But what is the hardest part of this all? You may think it's challenge packs, or Hush-Hush (which are unique riddles you have to solve from just a few hints and an image) medals, but the true *final boss* is Skill medals category, which was introduced on May 4th 2016. In fact, before the introduction of this category there were multiple people achieving 100% of medals, but after that only Dropinx managed to do such a feat, which makes their achievement so special!
